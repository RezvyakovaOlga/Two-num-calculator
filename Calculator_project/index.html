How to use this calculator? (click)
1. This calculator can perform operations only on two numbers;
2. There are common actions like addition (+), subtraction (-), multiplication (x), division (/), modul (|x|), degree (xn), factorial (n!). Feel free to use them without any hesitation!
3. Also there are some special actions: calculating the remainder of the division (%), calculating the whole part of the division (mod), any root (n√ x) - at first you need to enter the degree, click on the root button and then enter the num which root you want to find;
4. There are two transforming buttons. You can transform arabic numbers to roman (1 → I) and vice versa (I → 1);
5. And finally there are some convertional buttons. You can convert decimal numbers to Binary (Bin), Hexadecimal (Hexa) and back to decimal from these counting systems (Dec from Bin and Dec from Hexa);
6. You can enter your numbers and actions straight to the process line, but for your convenience you can switch between keyboards using the list on the right part of the window. But be attentive: almost all actions (with the exception of appropriate convertional or transforming actions) can be performed only on decimal numbers.
